# Code and Resources
The workflow integrates multiple open-source software packages and resources spanning neuroimaging preprocessing, normative modelling, community detection, genetic analysis, statistical inference, and data visualization.

---

## Neuroimaging Processing and Quality Control

- **FreeSurfer**  
  https://surfer.nmr.mgh.harvard.edu  
  Cortical surface reconstruction and anatomical parcellation.

- **iBEAT-V2**  
  https://github.com/iBEAT-V2/iBEAT-V2.0-Docker  
  Infant brain MRI preprocessing and surface reconstruction.

- **MRIQC**  
  https://github.com/nipreps/mriqc  
  Automated MRI quality assessment.

- **FSQC**  
  https://github.com/Deep-MI/fsqc  
  Quality control for FreeSurfer outputs.

---

## Normative Modelling and Subtyping

- **Lifespan BrainChart Framework**  
  https://github.com/brainchart/Lifespan  
  Normative modelling using GAMLSS.

- **GenLouvain**  
  https://github.com/GenLouvain/GenLouvain  
  Categorical multilayer community detection for subtype identification.

---

## Statistical and Genetic Analyses

- **SciPy**  
  https://scipy.org/  
  Statistical analyses.

- **PLINK 2.0**  
  https://www.cog-genomics.org/plink/2.0/  
  Genotype processing and quality control.

- **PRS-CSx**  
  https://github.com/getian107/PRScsx  
  Polygenic risk score estimation.

- **PyPLS**  
  https://github.com/netneurolab/pypyls  
  Partial least squares (PLS) analysis.

---

## Spatial and Biological Analyses

- **ENIGMA Toolbox**  
  https://github.com/MICA-MNI/ENIGMA  
  Disease epicenter mapping and multimodal spatial analyses.

- **Spin Test**  
  https://github.com/spin-test/spin-test  
  Spatial permutation testing.

- **Metascape**  
  https://metascape.org  
  Functional enrichment analysis.
